Passion video

Pilot project


This pilotproject is completed in a group.

In this project we had to produce a short video, about a person with a passion. We chose to film Gitte, who has a passion about her orangeri in her garden, which she calls "Driverhuset".

To produce the video we filmed an interview with and external microphone, and used different filming techniques like B-rolls, stablishingshot, over the shoulder and so on.

All video footage is edited and synchronized in Adobe Premiere Pro, and the sound from the external microphone is edited in audition.

Redesign of Sawasdee Wellness

Redesign


For this project we redesigned a webpage for a client. The final result was a handcoded responsive website, with different video and photo content. We also documented our whole progress, which is linked on the right.

To keep track of our progress we used scrumboard, burndownchart and github to keep all the files collected. These were very good methods to hand out different tasks and keep track of the work as a group.

For the analysation we used a BERT test and a 5-second test. These tests helped us analyse our content and functionality of the website.
