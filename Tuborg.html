Tuborg Nul

Campaign site and video


We had to develop a campaign for the new alcoholfree beer Tuborg Nul.
We were challenged to get really creative with our idea for this campaign, and therefor we used different models to fulfill our concept. We used Desk research, empathy maps, business canvas model and SWOT and TOWS.

Our final idea was that u can use this alcoholfree beer for more than just drinking, as a sign that u will always stay fresh and ready for your day with Tuborg nul.

Click the link on the right, to see our website and campaign video. This webside is made in Wordpress.
