From research to idea


In this protject i working with making a t-shirt webshop.

The primary focus for this webshop was alot of theory and how to use different models to study human behaviour when it comes to a consumers experience on a website. I working alot with understanding a row of disciplines that are required for a good user experience. Therefore I used reserchmethods to understand userbehavious, user needs and users motivation. These methods include Observation, interview and a long testphase, which lead to a prototype made in adobe XD.

After collecting qualitative an quantitative data, my idéa creating phase started. My idea resulted in creating a T-shirt shop that sells sustainable t-shirts that support the human nature and also plants a tree for every product bought in the webshop. After coming up with this idéa I started sketching the prototype by hand.

Final Prototype


From the idea I had gotten from the research phase, I have created a prototype of the website in adobe XD. To solve this prototype I have used AI, UX and UI to create a HIFI prototype. The design I've created is supposed look like an app.

To complete the project I used some methods I learned earlier to have an idéa of what i was creating. I had created moodboards, styile tiles and produced a simple wireframe that lead to my HIFI prototype.
